SMB Hungama Band
The Bollywood Spectacle
USP

Our flagship Livewire band since 2015, bringing unstoppable energy and unforgettable performances to every stage. 500+ electrifying shows and counting! SMB Hungama Band turns every event into a full-on celebration with power-packed live music, Bollywood vibes, and non-stop entertainment.

Booking essentials

Band Composition
  • 4 Vocalist-Performers
  • 1 DJ (vdjing)
  • 1 Percussionist
  • 1 drummer
  • 1 piano
  • 1 Manager
  • 1 Sound Engineer
Travel & Logistics

Travel mode will depend on venue distance and will be finalized with the client.

  • By Air (Long Distance):
    10 members (2 Business + 8 Economy). Client to book To & Fro flights with priority check-in, XL seats, meals, and 15 kg excess baggage.
  • By Train (Mid Distance):
    AC 2/3 Tier or Executive Class.
  • By Road (Short Distance):
    Comfortable tempo traveller / luxury vehicles.

All travel arrangements to be managed by the client.

Accommodation
  • 4 rooms to be arranged for band members.
  • Hotel/rooms should be at the event venue or within 1 km distance.
  • Accommodation must be comfortable and of good standard.
Tech Rider
To be arranged by Host/Organiser.
Sound Check
  • 2 hours required before the show for complete sound check and setup.
  • Stage, sound, and technical arrangements must be ready on time.
Performance Timeline
90-minute set, extendable to 120 minutes based on crowd response.
Performance Fee
₹2,00,000 + GST
Payment Terms
50% advance to confirm booking · 50% balance at sound check.
