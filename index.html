Manish Ahuja
Founder · Performance & Artist Mentoring

Fashion design background, business family roots, and a magnetism that was clearly born for the stage. Manish entertains, performs, and leads fearlessly — with the kind of taste in music that consistently meets the pulse of every age group in the room.

A sales and marketing genius. He effortlessly yet tirelessly leads the performance and artist mentoring division at Once More, switching seamlessly between fronting Tamasha and being the calm, calculating brain behind its success graph.

Patient, precise, detail-oriented in every decision — a hardworker unlike any other. He's a force in the industry, and one to look up to for Gen Zs and peers alike.

Richa Raina
Founder · Operations, Strategy & Creative

Lawyer by education, trained dancer and choreographer by passion, entrepreneur from every bone and cell. Richa knew at twelve she wanted to lead and build.

After being a sought-after name in luxury sangeet choreography, she doubled down on the hustle — juggling wedding planning and choreography until the undeniable pull toward artists, stage, glamour and performing arts brought her here. That choreographed chaos is now Once More Entertainment.

She leads operations, admin, people, finance, strategy, creative — everything that needs to keep the engine thriving and vibing. A relentless, unforgiving need to improve our offerings every day. The quiet, unseen force (until now) who fuels the heart of it all.
